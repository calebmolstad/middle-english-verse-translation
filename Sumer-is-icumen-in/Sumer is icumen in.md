###Sumer is icumen in
Sumer is icumen in,<br/>
Lhude sing, cuccu;<br/>
Groweth sed<br/>
and bloweth med,<br/>
And springth the wde nu;<br/>
Sing, cuccu!<br/>
Awe bleteth after lomb,<br/>
Lhouth after calue cu;<br/>
Bulluc sterteth,<br/>
Bucke uerteth,<br/>
Murie sing, cuccu!<br/>
Cuccu, cuccu,<br/>
Wel singes thu, cuccu;<br/>
Ne swic thu naver nu.<br/>
*Sing, cuccu, nu; sing, cuccu;*<br/>
*Sing, cuccu, nu; sing, cuccu;*
